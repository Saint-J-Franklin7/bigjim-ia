Prompt 01
Génération de site web client
Create a professional one-page website for [COMPANY NAME], a [SERVICE] based in [ADDRESS].

Business info:
- Services: [SERVICES]
- Phone: [PHONE]
- Email: [EMAIL]
- Tone: professional, minimalist and modern

Take inspiration from the design style in the attached image — adapt the aesthetic, layout energy and visual hierarchy to fit this business. Do not copy it, just use it as a creative direction.

Page structure (in order):
1. Hero — strong headline, subheadline, CTA button "Get a free quote"
2. Services — 3 to 4 cards
3. About — short trust-building paragraph
4. Contact — phone, email, simple form

Design directives:
- Mobile first, fully responsive
- Clean and modern typography
- High contrast CTA buttons
- Consistent color palette throughout
- Use clean and pro photos, no copyright (not too much)
Remplace les champs en orange par les infos de ton client avant d'envoyer le prompt. Joins une image de référence (screenshot d'un site que tu aimes) pour guider le style visuel. Trouve ton inspi sur dribbble.com.
